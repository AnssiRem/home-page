BIO

Anssi Remes – Game Developer

I am a junior game developer and a fresh graduate of Kajaani University of Applied Sciences. Just a few years ago I would not have believed I would be getting into the trade of making games, even when my earliest experiences at the age of fifteen with Flash 8’s action script and mapping with Hammer editor paved the way for it.

Video games have always fuelled my fire and perfectly combine all my interests: mathematics, graphical art, music and most importantly storytelling. In Kajaani I mained my studies in the programming of games, as I believe it’s the most fundamental part of interactive software development, yet I have taken every chance I have been given to learn designing the best games.

I was born in the rural town of Sodankylä, which I left only after graduating from the local high school and completing military service. Growing up I absorbed incredible amounts of video gaming insight playing everything I got my hands on. From dated hand-me-down 8-bit machines and MS-DOS games to the Xbox 360 games I bought when I started to earn my own wages. When I was not playing, I was loving winter sports or practicing with any metal band I was in at the time.

MY WORKS

Click on the images to enlarge

Thesis

In December 2020 I submitted my thesis on procedural level generation using cube marching. It is freely available on Theseus.fi, the document is in finnish.

The thesis involved me research on cube marching algorithm and programming an example script in Unity.

Dashbot Ninja

The project I worked on during the summer 2019. Game is about dashing through robotic enemies in varied hazardous enviroments combining casual fun and hardcore action. The game took part in Assembly Summer 2019's game dev competition placing 4th!

The game is available for download on on Steam and previously on Google Play!

I contributed game design and programming. Made with Unity.

Frog Out!

As it states on the Steam storepage: "Frog Out! is a wacky local multiplayer party game for up to 4 players." It features three different gamemodes, plenty of frog skins to choose from and wacky couch game fun.

Frog Out! is my first commercial release. I did programming in the game's development team working on live updates, mobile port and online play.

Made with Unreal Engine.

Ron Colt

The game was produced during the autumn 2019. In it you take the role of Ron Colt in a twisted stage play of the same name. Onstage mayhem and bizarre power-ups coming your way!

I was wholly dedicated to designing and producing the game.

Made with Unity.

Selene

Selene is a 2.5D puzzle exploration game focused on the eviromental storytelling and exploration. The game was in the works during the spring of 2019. I worked both as the story writer and the level & puzzle designer of the game.

Made with Unity.

Bumper Balls

A game I made solo practising AI scripting and network programming in 2019.

Bumper Balls features a client-server system, AI opponents and tranquil programmer art. I've made its GitHub repo public, check it out here!

Game client made with Unity and the independent server program with just Visual Studio.

Android game

A minimalistic experiment on mobile development during spring of 2019. The game utilizes mobile phone's camera, microphone and gyroscope.

Made with Android Studio.

Pluto

A vertical slice of a turn-based RPG featuring carnage and special abilites on six tactical lanes of combat. On Pluto I spent my time programming the core combat system. Made in 2018.

Made with Unity.
